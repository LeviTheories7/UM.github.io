Martin Luther

the German theologian and religious reformer, has written numerous books throughout his life.

  • The Bondage of the Will - A theological treatise in which Luther argues against the idea of free will and emphasizes the sovereignty of God in salvation
  • The Ninety-Five Theses- Luther's famous document that challenged the corruption of the Catholic Church and sparked the Protestant Reformation.
  • On the Freedom of a Christian- This book explores the relationship between faith and good works, emphasizing the concept of justification by faith alone.
  • Commentary on Galatians- Luther's commentary on the book of Galatians, providing insights into his understanding of justification and the gospel.
  • Table TalkA collection of conversations and anecdotes recorded by Luther's students, offering insights into his thoughts on various theological and practical matters.
